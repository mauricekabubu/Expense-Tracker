#EXPENSE-Tracker

Expense Tracker project is a web application using Flask python web framework that tracks your monthly expenses and displays it on your dashboard in form of a Chart/graph.
It clearly shows your expenses and your spending and checks if you reached your limit spending.
Has Ai integrated features to help and give you more tips on better ways to spend and invest your income growth.

Features:
1. Dashboard
2. Graphs
3. Charts
4. Tables
5. Forms
6. Ai integrated chatbots

 Expenses examples:
 1. Fees
 2. Income
 3. Salary
 4. Shopping
 5. Rent
 6. Transport
 7. Entertainment

Tips to start:
1. Create an account
2. Login using your accounts details
3. Setup your target month expense
4. Regulary check and update your expense per day
5. Use the tables to check your expenses

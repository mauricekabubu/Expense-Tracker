# Expense Tracker

## Overview
**Expense Tracker** is a web application built using **Python and Flask** that helps you track your monthly expenses.  
The application provides a **dashboard** with charts and graphs to visualize spending, monitor expenses, and check if you’ve reached your spending limits.  

It also includes **AI-integrated features** to provide tips and guidance on better ways to manage and invest your income.

---

## Features

- **Dashboard** – see an overview of your expenses  
- **Graphs and Charts** – visualize your spending trends  
- **Tables** – detailed record of expenses  
- **Forms** – add and edit expenses easily  
- **AI Chatbot** – get tips on smarter spending and investing  

---

## Expense Categories

Examples of tracked expenses:  

- Fees  
- Income  
- Salary  
- Shopping  
- Rent  
- Transport  
- Entertainment  

---

## How to Use

1. **Create an account**  
2. **Login** using your account details  
3. **Set up your target monthly expenses**  
4. **Regularly update your daily expenses**  
5. **Use tables and charts** to monitor your spending
